Variational Multiview Deconvolution

Energy Term

$$E(u) = \lambda R(u) + \sum_{j=1}^m T(u \ast k_j, f^j_\delta)$$
  • $f^j_\delta$ the $j$-th observation of the underlying image $f$.
  • $k_j$ the PSF of the optical system which acquired $f^j_\delta$.
  • $R(\cdot)$ the regularization term with parameter $\lambda$.
  • $T(\cdot, \cdot)$ the cost function.
  • $u$ the reconstructed image.

Regularization Terms

Total Variation [ROF92]:

$$TV(u) := \|\nabla u\|_{2,1}$$
  • Piece-wise consistency.
  • Stair-casing artifacts.

Figure x: A reconstruction result with TV as regularization term plotted as a surface, where the gray-scale intensity is represented by the height. In the bottom right corner the underlying ground truth with smooth intensity transitions.

Regularization Terms

TV-Huber [CP11]:

  • Huber-Norm [Hub73]:

$$\| x\|_{\alpha} := \begin{cases} \frac{\| x\|_2^2}{2\alpha} &, \| x\|_2 \leq \alpha\\ \| x\|_2 - \frac{\alpha}{2} &, \| x\|_2 > \alpha \end{cases}$$
  • $\ell_1$-norm is replaced by the Huber-Norm.

Figure x: A reconstruction result with TV as regularization term plotted as a surface, where the gray-scale intensity is represented by the height. In the bottom right corner the underlying ground truth with smooth intensity transitions.

Regularization Terms

Total Generalized Variation [BKL]:

  • $TV(u) := \|\nabla u\|_{2,1}$
  • Piece-wise consistency.
  • Stair-casing artifacts.
